    Milestone

    Congratulations! You made it to the end of Core!

    Before moving on to Fluency, let's see how much you can do.

    Below is a conversation between Ana and Kai. They are making travel plans by phone.

    This dialogue is more challenging and contains a few words you haven't learned yet, to mimic real language encounter. Try to understand from context first, then check the tips below after reading. You may understand more than you expect!

    1. Ana: Eon, Kai!
    Celi run i anifi en beilem?

    2. Kai: Nim i anifi lisoi.
    Nim i bejui e beitor litamar,
    eta i foum i gerina en beitan.

    3. Ana: Oihi iliora!
    Run i do i anisu e bejae en beitor?

    4. Kai: Ia, i anisu e yalgai bejae.

    5. Ana: Oifu!
    Su ce run i dai i anye litam?

    6. Kai: I dai i bevio en yani bedam,
    a ilhei i ilaluan ca oipili.
    En limel, i dai i mo.

    7. Ana: A bevio a oi!
    Nim i geldovear nime beivu,
    eta nim i do i gerina.
    Mai, i iranum i mo en limel.

    8. Kai: Nima i do i mo yalgai.
    Ce run i iliro, moa?

    9. Ana: Oila.
    Nima i do i mo e moa en bedam.
    Su i anvu en mo bevio litamis.

    10. Kai: Celi run i anocari?
    Nim i iran i eodya e run,
    liperis, i dami a eompela u falte.

    11. Ana: Nim i anelem en ligo lipardas,
    eta i do i eompela su run.
    Oi!
    I iran i eodani e yani ilhei,
    su i do i anefene cali en bo.

    12. Kai: Ia, nim i do i anefene cali faigel.
    Su cedom run?

    13. Ana: Nim i anifi en beilem en litegai.
    Eori beitan, i yasoi anvu!
    Su i fou i anvu en bosvi,
    i dami a bosvi en beilem?

    14. Kai: I dami, cadom bortal.
    A beilem a ho yalen.

    15. Ana: Oia!

    16. Kai: Oipoh!
    Nim i ilahai e oines lilon nima i mo!

    17. Ana: Bi oila a nim i anita e rune oimel!
    A run a oi lili su lili!

    18. Kai: Run su!
    A run a nime eofa bi oi.
    I anvu!

    19. Ana: I anvu!

    Tips

    a = subject marker
    a...a... = is/are
    a [subject] i [verb] e [direct complement] u [indirect complement/to/for]
    (an)do = can
    anefene = rest
    (ane)lem = stay, remain
    anifi = come
    anisu = carry, bring
    anita = take, receive
    anocari = leave
    anvu = move, go
    anye = make, do
    (ani)dai = want
    (ani)fou = need
    -ar = completion suffix (e.g., did, have done)
    beivu = car
    beilem = station
    beimete = bus, wagon
    beitor = bicycle
    bejae = luggage, gear
    bejui = rent, borrow
    bedam = plaza, square
    bevio = shop, store
    bo = house
    bortal = door
    bosvi = toilet
    ca = that, which (connector)
    cadom = where (connector)
    cali = when (connector)
    ce = what?
    celi = when?
    cedom = where?
    (da)iran = like
    dami = exist, there is
    e = direct complement marker
    en = in/at/on
    eodani = meet
    eodya = invite
    eompela = parade
    eofa = friend
    eon = hello
    eori = please
    eta = therefore
    faigel = marry, wedding, marriage
    geldove = sell
    gerina = money, pay, spend
    ho = opposite
    i = verb marker ia = yes
    ilaluan = say
    ilhei = people
    iliro = think
    -is = yet to start/prospective suffix (e.g., will, next)
    ligo = half
    lili = again
    lilon = while
    limel = evening
    lipardas = month
    liperi = week
    lisoi = soon
    litam = day
    litegai = minute
    mai = but
    mo = food, eat
    mo bevio = restaurant
    moa = fruit
    nim/nima/run/runa/hay/haya = personal pronouns
    nime/rune/haye... = possessives (pronoun + e)
    oifu = silly, trivial
    oi = fun, entertaining
    oia = yay
    oihi = interesting
    oila = happy, joy
    oimel = stories
    oines = joke, funny
    oipili = cute, adorable
    oipoh = exciting, excited
    su = and, with, also
    u = indirect complement marker / to, for
    -um = no, not (suffix)
    yasoi = fast
    yalgai = small
    yani = new

    Eta i foum i gerina en beitan.
    So I don't need to spend money on the bus.

    A ilhei i ilaluan ca oipili.
    People say that it's cute.

    Liperis, i dami a eompela u falte.
    There is a party for young people next week.

    I iran i eodani e yani ilhei, su i do i anefene cali en bo.
    I like to meet new people, and I can rest when I am home.

    Eori beitan, i yasoi anvu!
    Please bus, move fast!

    I dami, cadom bortal.
    There is, where the door (is).

    Nim i ilahai e oines lilon nima i mo.
    I will tell (you) a joke while we eat.

    Bi oila a nim i anita e rune oimel! A run a oi lili su lili!
    I'm happy that I take (listen to) your stories! You are again and again (always) amusing!

    Run su! A run a nime eofa bi oi. I anvu!
    You too! It's fun that you are my friend. Let's go!

    Answer Sheet

    b) They will eat at a restaurant tonight. UM / false

    c) Ana can go to the party. IA / true

    2. Sequence

    c) Go to the square (right after)

    a) Eat at a restaurant (tomorrow)

    b) Go to a party (next week)

    3. Open Questions

    a) Ce a Ana i iranum?

    Possible answer: A Ana i iranum i mo en limel.

    b) Cedom a bosvi i dami?

    Possible answer: A bosvi i dami en beilem cadom bortal.

    Dialogue Translation

    Ana: Hello, Kai! When will you arrive at the station?

    Kai: Hello! I will arrive soon. I rented a bicycle yesterday, so I don't need to spend money on the bus.

    Ana: It's an interesting idea! Can you carry luggage on the bike?

    Kai: Yes, I brought small luggage.

    Ana: Silly! And what do you want to do today?

    Kai: I want to shop at the new square, people say it's cute. At night, I want to eat.

    Ana: Shopping is fun! I sold my car, so I can spend. But I don't like to eat at night.

    Kai: We can eat something small. What do you think, fruit?

    Ana: Happy! We can eat fruit at the square. And go to the restaurant tomorrow.

    Kai: When do you leave? I want to invite you, there is a party for young people next week.

    Ana: I stay half a month, so I can party with you. Fun! I like meeting new people, and I can rest when I am home.

    Kai: Yes, I can rest when I marry. And where are you now?

    Ana: I will arrive at the station in a minute. Please bus, move fast. And I need to go to the bathroom, is there a bathroom at the station?

    Kai: There is, where the door is. It's a short station.

    Ana: Yes!

    Kai: Excited! I will tell you a joke while we eat!

    Ana: I am happy to listen to your stories. You are amusing again and again!

    Kai: You too! It's fun to have you as friend. Let's go!

    Ana: Let's go!
