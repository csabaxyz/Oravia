    Gradation

    Remember the NE cluster, which has function words related to quantity? NE does something else very important, too! We use ne+number to express the degree of something, and the scale always goes from 0 to 10. For example: 

    netor yuvabo = 2 out of 10 comfortable = a little comfortable
nealem yuvabo = 4 out of 10 comfortable = somewhat comfortable
neteva yuvabo = 6 out of 10 comfortable = rather comfortable
nedas yuvabo = 10 out of 10 comfortable = super comfortable

    So when we use ne+number in front of an adjective, we are talking about the intensity or quantity of that. Another example: 

    nesunya lufiva = 0 out of 10 cold = not cold at all
nedastam lufiva = 11 out of 10 cold = too cold

    We can pair it with other words besides adjectives. For example, lirul: 

    nesunya lirul = 0 out of 10 habit = never
nepar lirul = 3 out of 10 habit = sometimes
nealui lirul = 5 out of 10 habit = half the time
neauta lirul = 8 out of 10 habit = often
nedas lirul = 10 out of 10 habit = every time

    Or, ilie: 

    nesunya ilie = 0 out of 10 likelihood = impossible
nepar ilie = 3 out of 10 likelihood = possibly
nealui ilie = 5 out of 10 likelihood = 50/50
neperi ilie = 7 out of 10 likelihood = likely
neauta ilie = 8 out of 10 likelihood = probably
nedas ilie = 10 out of 10 likelihood = certainly

    You can apply this to any gradable dimension, really.

    To sum up:

    ne + number = gradation out of 10
ne + number [adjective] = intensity [adjective]
ne + number lirul = frequency
ne + number ilie = likelihood

    Now try to create 3 sentences using ne+number.

    What do you think LEA and LEI mean?

    LEA means landscape, and LEI means plant.

    And here are some other meanings:

    Oravia English
    ledom land, territory
    levi fertile, nutrient, fertilizer
    leno soil, dirt, clay
    lenodur rock, stone
    leafou desert, to lack
    leamoma farm, to cultivate
    leayo garden, park
    lealei forest, jungle
    leipili flower
    leinou grass, lawn
    leini seed
    leirih tree, wood
    leide root, tuber

    Ce yo leipili run i dairan?

    En rune bo i dami a leayo dou leinou?
