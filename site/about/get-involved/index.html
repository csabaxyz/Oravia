    Get Involved

    Interested in contributing to Oravia's development? I'm always looking for beta testers and feedback!

    Beta Testing

    Beta Testers give feedback on the language and materials. They are part of a cohort taking the course together, and tell us what works and what doesn't. They help shape the language development and the community. Beta Testers get a small stipend for their participation. No prior experience with constructed languages is needed.
