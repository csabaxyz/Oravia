# About Me

Hello! I am excited to present you Oravia, a constructed language. I like to classify it as an auxlang because my goal for Oravia is for it to be easy to learn and use. It is also a little bit of an artlang because it has unique features for creative expression, flexibility and personalization.

I am a social psychologist from Brazil and I love languages. I've studied 8 natural languages and dabbled in many others. This extensive experience as a researcher and as a language learner has helped shape Oravia.

I'd like to express my gratitude to the whole auxlang and conlang communities, and to all the constructed languages that inspired me, such as Mini-Linga, Toki Pona, Kotava, and Esperanto.

And to you as well who visited my website, thank you!
